Pad Thai

A bright, savory-sweet stir-fry with chewy rice noodles, tamarind, lime, and crunchy peanuts.

Prep 15 min
Cook 10 min
Serves 2

Sauce profile

  • Sweet from palm sugar
  • Sharp from tamarind and lime
  • Deep saltiness from fish sauce
